Our Mission

To provide highly trained, ethically sound, and professionally managed security personnel that safeguard people, property, and business operations — delivered with integrity, transparency, and consistent excellence across every deployment.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and technologically advanced security manpower company — creating a safer society through world-class guard training, ethical practices, and innovation-driven security solutions for every sector.

Our Story

18 Years of Protecting India

Founded in 2006 in Chennai, SecureForce began with 50 guards and a single corporate client. Today, we operate across 24 states with over 5,200 active deployments — all while maintaining the same founding principles: integrity, discipline, and accountability.

We have grown through repeat business and referrals — a testament to the trust our clients place in us. Our in-house training academy, centralized control room, and dedicated HR team make us self-sufficient and scalable at every level.
